Creamy Chicken

Description

This easy to make creamy chicken pasta will have you longing for more! A budget meal perfect for bulking, this meal packs about 1000kcal and 115g protein!

Ingredients

  • 300g chicken
  • 125g whole grain pasta
  • 100g philadelphia cheese
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Cut chicken into pieces, and start boiling water.
  2. Add chicken to medium-high heat pan. Add seasonings.
  3. When water is boiling, add pasta and boil for 10 minutes.
  4. When chicken is cooked, add philadelphia and some pasta water. Stir.
  5. When pasta is done, drain it and add to the pan with the chicken. Stir it until the sauce coats the pasta.
  6. Enjoy!
