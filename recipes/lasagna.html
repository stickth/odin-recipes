Lasagna

Description

This quick and easy lasagna will blow your tastebuds away! Based on authentic italian cooking, impress all your friends and family with this simple to follow recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 jar spaghetti sauce
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 32 ounces cottage cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 teaspoons dried parsley
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1/2 cup water

Steps

  1. Gather all ingridients and preheat oven to 175C
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir the ground beef until browned, approx 8-10 mins. Drain and discard grease. Stir in spaghetti sauce and simmer for 5 mins.
  3. Combine cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozarella cheese, eggs, 1/2 of the grated parmesan cheese, dried parsley, salt and pepper in a large bowl.
  4. Spread 3/4 cup of sauce in 9x13-inch baking dish. Cover with 3 uncooked lasagna noodles, 1 3/4 cups of cheese mixture, and 1/4 cup sauce; repeat layers once more. Top with remaining 3 noodles, sauce, mozzarella and parmesan cheese.
    Pour 1/2 cup water along edges of the dish. Cover tightly with aluminum foil.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 45 min. Uncover and bake for an additional 10 mins. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
  6. Enjoy!
