Salmon

Description

This delicious creamy salmon recipe tastes wonderful and is an excellent bulk meal. This healthy meal contains 1300+ kcals and 72 proteins! You are sure to impress someone with this!

Ingredients

  • 250g salmon
  • 150g heavy cream
  • 5g garlic
  • 20g cheese
  • 50g saladmix
  • 1/2 onion
  • 125g whole grain pasta

Steps

  1. Cut the onion.
  2. Add salmon to medium-high heat pan.
  3. Boil water.
  4. When salmon is almost cooked add the cut onion to the pan.
  5. Add pasta to boiling water and boil for 10 mins.
  6. Add the heavy cream to the pan and stir.
  7. Add the finished pasta to the pan and stir again until the sauce coats the pasta and salmon.
  8. Enjoy!
